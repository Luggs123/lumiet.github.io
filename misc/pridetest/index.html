PRIDE at UTD Socials

Pride at UTD serves as a safe social forum for members of the community and allies to meet and discuss thoughts, ideas, and issues concerning sexual orientation and gender identity. Our Discord server is a great way to get in touch with the community at any time, or you can check out our events tab for our upcoming meetings. We hope to see you around!
